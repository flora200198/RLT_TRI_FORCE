1. Selection Process for TNFUSRC - Forest Guard
The Selection Process for TNFUSRC - Forest Guard are :-
  1. Written Examination
  2. Physical Measurement Test
2. Educational Qualification for TNFUSRC - Forest Guard
TNFUSRC GUARD:
  • Complete +2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Zoology (or) Botany
TNFUSRC GUARD WITH LISENCE:
  • Passed Higher Secondary Courses with Physics/Chemistry/Biology/Zoology/Botany as one of the Subjects.
  • Valid driving license issued by the competent Transport Authority.
  • Knowledge of the mechanism of automobiles.
